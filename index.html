DR.BABASAHEB

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Chairman, Drafting Committee of the Indian Constitution with other members on Aug. 29, 1947

Former Minister of Law and Justice of India

Life Of Ambedkar So far

  1. Born: 14 April, 1891

  2. Place of Birth: Mhow in Central Provinces (currently Madhya Pradesh)

  3. Parents: Ramji Maloji Sakpal (father) and Bhimabai Murbadkar Sakpal (mother)

  4. Spouse: Ramabai Ambedkar (1906-1935); Dr. Sharada Kabir rechristened Savita Ambedkar (1948-1956)

  5. Education: Elphinstone High School, University of Bombay, Columbia University, London School of Economics

  6. Associations: Samata Sainik Dal, Independent Labour Party, Scheduled Castes Federation

  7. Political Ideology: Right winged; Equalism

  8. Religious Beliefs: Hinduism by birth; Buddhism 1956 onwards

  9. Publications: Essays on Untouchables and Untouchability, The Annihilation of Caste, Waiting for a Visa

  10. Profession: Jurist, economist, academic, politician, social reformer, anthropologist, writer

  11. Awards: Bharat Ratna

  12. Passed Away: 6, December, 1956
